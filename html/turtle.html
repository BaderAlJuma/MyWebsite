The Wise Turtle Do you start projects your never finish? Do you feel like no progress is being made towards your goals? Maybe you don’t have enough time? If yes, then good. It means you can improve. This is what you need to do: Write down goals. If you have a full time job pick no more than two. Otherwise pick three. Every single night (and I mean EVERY SINGLE NIGHT) before sleeping, write down a number for each goal: 3 if you performed above average. 2 for average. 1 below. 0 means you did nothing. Achieve goals. You’re welcome. Why does this method work? Answer: Your brain doesn’t like seeing 0s and 1s on that piece of paper. Very simple.
